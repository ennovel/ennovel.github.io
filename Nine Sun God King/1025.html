Chapter 1025

Flame Sun sect master very worriedly looked at the elders only to see them shaking their heads.“We should give up, no need to struggle!” an elder said with sigh.“Indeed, we have not competed with them for a long time, I didn’t expect that our disparity would be so big!”“Sect master, cede a portion of area to them. Our Heavenly Mountain have many uninhabited regions anyways!”“Their Golden Sun Sect won’t be able to bring in too many people and won’t be able to take away a large portion of area.”All the senior figures of the Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain are in favor of conceding.Xiao Yuemei said in a delicate voice : “Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s Inscription level has been lacking from the beginning, no wonder Xu Xiaoba is so weak! The competition is not yet over but you are already talking about handing over territory to the opponents, truly a bunch of losers!”Xu Xiaoba shouted : “What does low Inscription level have to do with my strength?”“There is a big relation! I don’t feel like explaining to you!” Xiao Yuemei said.“Pagoda master Qin, aren’t you very confident in your Inscription skill? Why don’t you go up and try?” an elder mockingly said : “Don’t worry, we won’t laugh at you when you lose. We never thought that you are actually skilled!”“Oh that’s right, if you win, Golden Sun Sect will give us two Immortal tools, we will give you one of them!”“How can he even win? Golden Sun Sect’s Inscription Masters have come prepared, they are determined to win, strange thing is that how confident we were before!”Some elders of the Flame Sun Sect are talking in low voice and shaking their heads.Qin Yun looked at Flame Sun sect master and said : “Sign a soul contract with me that if I win, you will give me one of the Immortal tools!”Xu Xiaoba said : “Why do we need to sign soul contract with you?”Xiao Yuemei mockingly said : “Idiot, you basically have no hope of winning! If you sign soul contract with my brother, he will win and then you won’t have to share your land with others and on top of that, you will even get an Immortal tool!”Xu Lun nodded towards Flame Sun sect master.“Alright!” Flame Sun sect master can only give it a shot. He signed a soul contract with Qin Yun.At this time, Qin Yun walked out of the crowd and said : “I will compete in the 2nd round!”“Who are you?” Jin Xuanhu saw how young Qin Yun is and his expression showed a touch of disdain.“He.. he is a tea delivery boy!” Jin Jingwei said with sneer : “5th uncle, he is just a trash.. ha ha.. Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s Inscription Masters must be very scared of losing to you and losing face, that is why they sent up this guy!”Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples recognized Qin Yun and they began shaking their head.“Sec master, what is going on? How can this kind of guy go up?”“Indeed, this guy even placed a cursed on our Heavenly Mountain to make it explode!”“He is so young, is he really skilled in Inscription?”“I heard he is from Xuan Domain.”“Xuan Domain is far backwards than Transcending Xuan Sea Region, how can his Inscription level be so high?”Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples don’t know anything about Qin Yun, they only know that he defeated Xu Xiaoba. Now even Golden Sun Sect disciples are criticizing Qin Yun along with Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples, they all feel that it is very inappropriate for Qin Yun to go up to compete.Jin Jingwei laughed loudly and said : “Flame Sun sect master, quickly have this guy scram back, letting him compete with my 5th uncle is an insult to my 5th uncle!”“Sect master, quickly let him come down, he is throwing away our face!”“No matter what, even if we lose, we can’t lose our dignity!”“Even if we can’t compete, we can not let this outsider go up, this is humiliating to our Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain!”Flame Sun sect master did not expect that so many disciples would be upset to see Qin Yun go up. Actually it is because Qin Yun defeated Xu Xiaoba. Although Xu Xiaoba is very rude and unreasonable, they still want to fawn upon him. So they are insulting Qin Yun to show Xu Xiaoba that they are taking revenge for him. On top of that, Qin Yun is an outsider and also very young, yet Xu Lun is very respectful to him. This caused them to be very jealous. Since they never saw Qin Yun’s skill with their own eyes, many young disciples and elders of Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain don’t like Qin Yun very much.Flame Sun sect master said to Qin Yun : “Pagoda master Qin, let’s not compete.”“Quickly come back, don’t lose face for our Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain!”“Our Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain don’t have this kind of person, quickly scram back!”“How high is your skill? Even our elders don’t dare to go up to compete yet you have such thick face! Truly overestimating your capability without any sense of shame!”While these young people are loudly jeering, an old man from the Golden Sun Sect’s side came out of the crowd. He is precisely Jin He.Jin He walked over and after carefully looking, he yelled : “Venerable Master, it’s really you! How come you are here? Jin He pays respect to Venerable Master!”Everyone immediately became quiet. Everyone knows very well about Jin He’s ability. Even if he were to be placed in Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain, a Great Master like him would have very high position. Yet now, Jin He is very respectfully bowing and greeting Qin Yun. This is a show of incomparable respect.Jin Jingwei also wanted to bring up the matter of Gu Nainai but seeing Jin He’s action, he swallowed back his words.Those elders of Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain who don’t like Qin Yun, are now completely dumbstruck. They all know that Qin Yun defeated Jin He but they all believed that Qin Yun was just lucky and it was a coincidence. But now seeing how respectful Jin He is towards Qin Yun, they know that Jin He completely acknowledges Qin Yun’s skill.Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples who were insulting and yelling at Qin Yun just earlier, have now became completely quiet. They have expression of disbelief, they did not expect Jin He to salute Qin Yun, that is how younger generations pay respect towards older generation! Qin Yun is not Jin He’s elder but since his ability his higher than Jin He’s, Jin He is very respectful towards Qin Yun.Xiao Yuemei’s expression is filled with pride as she glared at Xu Xiaoba. Xu Xiaoba is also extremely apprehensive, he also feels very sad in his heart because not only Qin Yun is stronger than him, his Inscription ability is also very strong.Xu Lun is now very certain that he did not make wrong choice.After a period of quiet, Golden Sun Sect’s people burst like a boiling pot!Jin Xuanhu originally looked at Qin Yun disdainfully. But now when he is looking at Qin Yun, there is a touch of respect in his gaze. He said : “Is your distinguished self the person who carved rune with 11th rank finesse, Qin Yun?”“It is indeed my humble self. I am now representing Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain to compete against you. I ask that you give me many pointers.” Qin Yun respectfully said and cupped his fists towards Jin Xuanhu.“Alright. A He competed a few rounds with you, he has wholeheartedly accepted his defeat. After coming back, he spoke about you many times. Now I am much obliged to see your distinguished self’s ability with my own eyes!” Jin Xuanhu also cupped his fists towards Qin Yun and said : “Today I will defeat you in A He’s place and take revenge!”“5th brother, Venerable Master is very powerful! You must not underestimate him!” Jin He said in low voice.“He is your Venerable Master, he has yet to defeat me!” Jin Xuanhu is also not particularly convinced by Qin Yun, he wants to personally experience Qin Yun’s ability to judge.Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples now have very unsightly expression because just earlier they offended a Venerable Master like Qin Yun.“Qin Yun, you are the younger generation, so you will choose the content of next competition! But we can not compete in the level of finesse!” Jin Xuanhu said. He is putting up this condition because his finesse level is same as Jin He.“Can I think a bit?” Qin Yun asked.“Sure, I also want to properly rest. When I competed earlier, I consumed a lot of my energy. I ask that you give me an hour to rest!” Jin Xuanhu said.Just earlier Jin Xuanhu was not like this. He was filled with confidence and even taunting Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain to send someone up. But now that he has seen Qin Yun coming to compete, he immediately wants to rest.Jin Jingwei feels that his heart is bleeding because he now realizes that he handed over 10 best quality Regent Origin Stones in vain. Now he has also realized that Qin Yun is not some tea delivery boy, he is actually the pagoda master of Star Traveling Pagoda in Hundred Pagoda Sect! Back then, he was together with Shen Bajian because his position is extremely high and equal to Shen Bajian. At this time he also saw Xiao Yuemei who is smiling at him.“Damn girl.. this Gu Nainai…” Jin Jingwei cursed in his heart but suddenly he also realized something and began pondering in his heart : “Gu Nainai(How can this old one help).. Gu Nainai.. Gu Nainai(Great Aunt)? Nainai(how can one help), Nainai(grandma)? Motherfucker! This young master has been swindled!”Flame Sun sect master said in low voice : “Old Xu, this pagoda master Qin is indeed a very rare genius!”Xu Lun said with sigh : “I already informed you all about his capabilities but you did not believe me!”An elder cursed in low voice : “Isn’t it because your grandson Xiaoba spreading rumors everywhere?”A few elders repeatedly nodded their heads and then looked at Xu Xiaoba.“This damned grandson!” Xu Lun said mockingly.Xu Xiaoba feels that he is leaning on a boiling pot. He said in a very bad mood : “You bunch of old farts who have lived for countless years but only know how belch and fart, you believe everything I say? Then I am telling you, dog shit is very good to eat! Why don’t you go eat it?”“You little shit.. believe or not, I will beat you up!” an elder cursed.“Come, come beat me up! You beat me up, I will beat up your grandson!” Xu Xiaoba fearlessly said : “Your grandson is also my little brother. If you dare to beat me up, I will tell him to bite you to death!”Xiao Yuemei finds this situation very hilarious. This Xu Xiaoba(little tyrant) is truly a little tyrant inside the Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain. Even the elders don’t know how to deal with him.“Little shameless puppy, you should personally bite people! Your bite should be more vicious!” Xiao Yuemei said with delicate smile.“Little girl, your brother will be defeated by Jin Xuanhu in a few moments, yet you are still speaking to me without worry?” Xu Xiaoba furiously said.“If my brother loses, the one suffering will be your Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain. Why should I be worried?” Xiao Yuemei said with charming smile : “Little shameless puppy, this young lady wants to bet with you. I bet that my brother will win! Do you dare to bet?”“Scram! I won’t bet with you! You and your brother are the same, full of evil tricks! I only bet with honest people!” Xu Xiaoba lost to Xiao Yuemei before. He now knows that Xiao Yuemei has many tricks up her sleeves. Although he is very thick skinned, he doesn’t want to be fooled again.Flame Sun Heavenly Mountain’s disciples and elders now greatly admires Xiao Yuemei because she actually dares to call Xu Xiaoba ‘little shameless puppy’! If they spoke such words, they would have terrible bad luck! They are also cursing Xu Xiaoba in their hearts because for 2 days Xu Xiaoba has been spreading rumor that Qin Yun is just a trash who relies on tricks to cheat people. They all believed his words.
