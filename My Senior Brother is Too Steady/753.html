Chapter 753: The Purple Cloud Palace!

Translator: 

Atlas Studios 

 

Editor: 

Atlas StudiosWhat happened in this world?Li Changshou rode impostor No. 3, the old Daoist priest of the human race in the Heaven Immortal realm, and stood on the peak of a desolate mountain in the East Continent. He looked at the surging gray aura that gathered above his head.The Heavenly Punishment was about to descend!

What exactly happened here?

The beautiful monkey king of Huaguo Mountain was conferred the title of the Great Sage, Heaven’s Equal.Most of the immortals and gods of the Heavenly Courts had some complaints about that. Youqin Xuanya admonished the Lingxiao Treasure Hall and argued with the fake Taibai Jinxing. She caused the Dao Ancestor to be alarmed because there was a slight variable in the Journey to the West Tribulation that had not been predicted by the Heavenly Dao.The Dao Ancestor checked the three thousand Great Dao carefully and sensed the traces of the Heavenly Fiend Dao. He went to the Reincarnation Pagoda to destroy the Heavenly Fiend Supremacy’s spiritual core and sensed Li Changshou’s plan.Anyone would be angry when they encountered such a situation.Anyone who thought that they had won, that their opponent had fled to the Chaos Sea in a sorry state, and that they had enjoyed the “thrill of victory” for a thousand years would realize that it was just the complete set of their opponent. They would more or less feel strange.The Dao Ancestor was like that.Everything was developing in a direction that he could not control. The one who escaped was no longer an existence that could not be erased from the Great Dao rules.He had long been schemed against by Li Changshou!In order to take back the initiative, the Dao Ancestor chose to destroy the script and began a series of operations.Sun Wukong’s mind was controlled to enter the Tushita Palace by mistake. He consumed the Spirit Pills in the Tushita Palace and was thrown into Huaguo Mountain by the Heavenly Dao to hide.The Dao Ancestor directly controlled the minds of the remaining experts of the Demon Court and gathered them in Huaguo Mountain.In just three days, Huaguo Mountain was bustling.The Heavenly Soldiers surged over, wanting to destroy the demons of Huaguo Mountain and launch a thunderous attack.The battle at Huaguo Mountain was intense. A large number of demon experts were destroyed.The fake Taibai Jinxing suddenly appeared and used the Jade Emperor’s orders to kill the monkey living beings under Sun Wukong’s tent.The Dao Ancestor’s actions were meant to incite the battle between the Sun Wukong and the Heavenly Courts. He wanted to trigger the greatest variable of the Western Sect’s calamity.The Dao Ancestor’s logic was actually not complicated. The only time that the Earth could impact the Heavenly Dao and cause slight turbulence in the Primordial World was when the Great Sage, Heaven’s Equal created a huge commotion in the Heaven Palace. Li Changshou was most likely to attack at that moment.The Dao Ancestor had disrupted his steps because he wanted to disrupt Li Changshou’s plan and force him to appear.The effect was quite obvious. Li Changshou stood up.However, this was the Dao Ancestor’s “lucky hit”.Li Changshou stood up because he did not want his disciple to be sad after today.How could he not understand the Dao Ancestor’s plan?However, since things had come to this, he could only accept the challenge.The last chance of victory that he could grasp still existed in the hands of the Dao Ancestor.This was a battle that concerned countless living beings in the Three Realms. He had to be extremely stable. He could not afford to make any mistakes.However, it was also a battle that concerned his disciple’s future.There was no need for the two of them to distinguish who was more important. Li Changshou did not want to use Sun Wukong’s sacrifice to obtain victory in the entire battle.The so-called natural tragedy was just an excuse that he could not evade.If he could not even protect a disciple, how could he say that he could protect all living beings in the world in front of the selfish desires of the Heavenly Dao?This time!Li Changshou would no longer let the Heavenly Dao destroy Jie School like last time. He would not let the Dao Ancestor seal his teacher and let the Dao Ancestor bully the living beings related to him!No. 3 took the initiative to appear and looked up at the Purple Cloud Palace. He used the Dao runes of the balanced Great Dao to attract the Dao Ancestor’s attention.He cupped his hands and almost shouted, “Fellow Daoist.”The Dao Ancestor’s response to him was straightforward and decisive. There was not a hint of Dao runes revealed. However, he directly mobilized the power of the Heavenly Dao that could not be calculated and turned into Tribulation Clouds that gathered towards No. 3’s fake body.In just an instant!The Great Dao of Equalization that was revealed to the world.The old Daoist priest stood on the mountain peak with his hands behind his back.The endless Tribulation Cloud was already brewing the purple-black Divine Lightning…It attracted the attention of the strongest living beings in the world!In the Goddess’s Palace, Goddess Nuwa, who was reading by the pool, flashed and appeared in front of the pavilion. She looked at Li Changshou.In the Lingxiao Hall, the Heavenly Emperor, who was dressed in a yellow robe and wearing a crown, stood up. He stared at the jade table in front of him and trembled.In the Primordial Battle Palace, Zhao Gongming pushed the immortal of Jie School in front of him away and glared at the sea of clouds below.At the hiding place of the fake Li Changshou, who was in the spiritual mountain, Saint Jie Yin had already disappeared. The chanting in the hall of Buddha Duobao stopped.In the small courtyard of the three friends behind the Jade Void Palace, the middle-aged Daoist who was lying under the tree paused for a while. Then, he smiled and shook his head gently.That figure.It was an unfamiliar figure that seemed to be able to contain the world.The Grand Pure One…“Changgeng!”“Changshou?”However, no one knew why Li Changshou had suddenly appeared.Even the Dao Ancestor, who reacted the fastest, could not understand why Li Changshou would choose such an opportunity to reveal his tracks.In the Dao Ancestor’s opinion, Li Changshou’s actions were clearly not rational. He did not have a specific goal. It was not a good time for Li Changshou.That was what Li Changshou had told the Dao Ancestor previously. There was a fundamental difference in their perspective of looking at the problem.Impersonator No. 2 secretly escaped into Huaguo Mountain.Taking advantage of the chaotic battle everywhere, Li Jing’s pagoda in the sky kept spinning, and the Heavenly Courts had yet to remove the protective array formation, he rushed to the place where the monkey soldiers were gathered.Every monkey spirit that had a special negative karma surrounding the Heavenly Dao was a monkey living being that Sun Wukong had killed in the Book of Life and Death in the Netherworld.Specks of starlight flew out of Li Changshou’s sleeve. Miniature array discs that the fake body had secretly made scattered from the ground. In the blink of an eye, they formed 72 Teleportation Arrays.The arrays were connected and the Dharma was connected.In the light beam that fell from the Heavenly Courts’ protective array, the monkeys were either stunned, stunned, or eager to fight the Heavenly Courts’ massacre with their fellow demons. They also wanted to escape but were trapped by the light beam.At that moment, immortal light that was like vines wrapped around the monkey’s ankles.The Cosmic Dao runes spread out.In the sky above Huaguo Mountain, Li Jing stared at Taibai Jinxing in front of him. His calm face revealed some confusion.Li Jing asked in confusion, “Why would a mere monkey attract His Majesty’s attention? Lord Taibai Jinxing, what are you trying to do?”“Marshal Li Jing.” The fake Taibai Jinxing smiled and said, “This is just a wrong judgment caused by Sun Wukong.Previously, they had always thought that Sun Wukong was a divine stone that Goddess Nuwa had refined. Therefore, they treated him as a material that could be made. However, the marshals of the Heavenly Courts were dissatisfied. They could only kill Sun Wukong to appease everyone’s anger.These monkeys have destroyed the order of reincarnation. They are the evil consequences that Sun Wukong has caused. They should be killed.”Li Jing frowned and wanted to speak, but his beard moved.The fake Taibai Jinxing looked at the place where Li Changshou’s fake body was and smiled. “Marshal Li Jing, don’t hesitate. I will bear this matter alone.”“Isn’t it too disrespectful to treat these demons as living beings?”Li Jing said in a low voice, “Although the demons are ancient remnant races, there are many cruel, murderous, and evil people among them. However, most of the lesser demons with low cultivation levels are no different from the fairies in the mountain. The monkeys are the same. The Heavenly Courts were born to protect the Three Realms and protect the weak from the strong. Is it against the Heavenly Courts’ intention to treat these living beings like this? Lord Taibai Jinxing, why don’t you imprison the monkey subordinates in the General’s Pagoda first? Later, we will go to the Lingxiao Hall. If His Majesty orders to kill them, I will personally destroy their souls. Is that okay?”“No.” The smile on the fake Taibai Jinxing’s face gradually disappeared. Just as he was about to speak, a strange Dao rune suddenly erupted under his feet.Li Jing and the fake Taibai Jinxing looked down at the same time. They saw that there was no sign of the monkey among the beams of light that the Heavenly Soldiers’ protective array had shot down.Only a few monkey feathers floated gently.Li Jing was stunned. The fake Taibai Jinxing sneered and said, “Cosmic Movement, Mustard Seed Cosmos. So that’s what you did, Fellow Daoist. What a good technique.”At that moment, the fake Taibai Jinxing passed Li Jing. His body emitted a rich heavenly might, and the five-pointed star on his forehead shone brightly.He flicked the horsetail whisk in his hand gently and was placed in the “place of birth”. Sun Wukong, who was about to wake up, fell asleep again.Then, he took out a jade token from his sleeve and scattered it in front of him.The talismans engraved on the jade token shone brightly. A rich spiritual energy sea surged like a tide, condensing into dark red mountains on the fake white head.The fake Taibai Jinxing’s voice spread everywhere.“Heavenly Soldiers, return to the array.”Just as Li Jing was about to speak, he realized that he could not open his mouth or move. His essence soul had been imprisoned.The fake Taibai Jinxing raised its left hand high and its wide sleeve fell. Its wrinkled palm supported the world.“By the orders of the heavens, we are here to destroy the demons of Huaguo Mountain.”Before he finished speaking, his left hand had already slid down casually.Behind him, the hundreds of mountains smashed towards Huaguo Mountain. Dozens of sun stars seemed to have appeared in the sky, and surging heat waves surged between heaven and earth.Countless Heavenly Soldiers and Heavenly Generals watched with twitching eyelids.On Huaguo Mountain, seven flags fluttered in the wind. Countless demons looked up at the apocalypse, their eyes filled with despair.Li Changshou watched that scene and felt a little helpless.That was the Dao Ancestor.He could always go personally without any scruples. Even if he had to deal with some incomplete demon tribes, he could openly use ten or a hundred times the spiritual energy of their strength.The Heavenly Fire Meteor.The main peak of Huaguo Mountain collapsed!In such a magnificent scene, living beings were howling, mourning, and calling out in despair.“The world is finally not so quiet.”However, more gazes shifted away from that place to the unknown mountain tens of thousands of kilometers away.A chuckle sounded in the world. Every Qi Refinement cultivator above the Zenith Heaven realm could clearly sense the Dao runes of the Great Dao of Equalization.Then, a clear voice sounded from all over the East Continent.“I’m Li Changshou, a disciple of the Dao Sect. I would like to meet the Dao Ancestor.”What answered him was the rumbling of the Tribulation Cloud above his head.Li Changshou muttered, “It seems that I have to think of a way to resist the Heavenly Tribulation.”As soon as he finished speaking, a bolt of purple-black lightning as thick as a bucket descended from the sky. It almost instantly shattered half of Li Changshou’s fake body and turned into gray smoke that dissipated.Li Changshou did not move at all. The old Daoist priest’s remnant body slowly collapsed. However, another old Daoist priest flew out of the old Daoist priest’s right sleeve.How could there be no backup for the Heavenly Fiend technique?“Dao Ancestor, don’t you want to talk to me again?”Before he finished speaking, another bolt of blackish-purple lightning struck. In the blink of an eye, it destroyed the fake body without leaving any traces.A deep hole appeared in the middle of the desolate mountain below. In the deepest part, dark red magma could be seen.Another chuckle sounded. A middle-aged Daoist walked out of the forest on the left and looked at the Tribulation Cloud in the sky with his hands behind his back.In the Purple Cloud Palace, the Dao Ancestor’s face collapsed.Li Changshou did not feel good about the Kun Peng Mystic Realm. He forcefully experienced the feeling of being “destroyed” twice.Most of the Qi Refinement cultivators with gaps in their Dao hearts had done great evil previously and did not receive retribution. Li Changshou could be considered to have used the Dao Ancestor to enforce justice for the heavens.Although he did not respect them and could not “disturb” them when their Dao hearts were occupied by him, he could not do anything about the special situation. He could only take his time to consider.Li Changshou looked up at the Tribulation Cloud in the sky and said calmly, “Could it be that the Dao Ancestor wants me to attack the Purple Cloud Palace?”This was the second move, a bluff.As if to verify his words, waves of spiritual energy suddenly came from the depths of the ground.The spiritual energy fluctuations were very weak. At first, they might only be like sand. However, soon, the sand turned into stones, and the stones turned into mountains.With the Immortal Du Sect as the center, the spiritual energy in a radius of thousands of kilometers suddenly surged!In the forest, figures walked out of the tree trunks.In the mountain stream and river, paper effigies floated to the surface of the water and quickly became the size of a normal person. Their eyes were listless as they flew out of the water.The valley, the abandoned mineral veins, the forest, and even the earth meridians!Light spots lit up one after another as figures stood up!There were both men and women. Their expressions were repeated. Previously, they were like gravel and withered wood. At that moment, they did not have any spirituality.The paper effigy self-destructed.At the place where the Tribulation Cloud was gathered, Li Changshou waved his sleeve, and the Cosmic Break Cave appeared below the Tribulation Cloud.There were more than a hundred of them!Specks of light surged out of the cave. Hundreds, thousands, tens of thousands of figures flew out densely!With mottled spiritual energy that was comparable to that of a Heaven Immortal realm Qi Refinement cultivator, and most of the repetitive paint, they pierced into the Tribulation Cloud like countless flying insects!Lightning roared as the power of the Heavenly Dao surged.However, there were too many paper effigies that suddenly appeared. The Tribulation Cloud could not react in time.The paper effigies that pounced from all directions embedded themselves in the Tribulation Cloud in the shortest time possible. They did not give the Tribulation Cloud a chance to erupt. They activated their talismans together, and the accumulated spiritual energy inside surged!Explode!Li Changshou stood in front of the forest with his hands behind his back. He looked at the scattered Tribulation Cloud and smiled.A ray of sunlight landed on him and was rather warm.It was difficult for the Tribulation Cloud to gather. Li Changshou calmly opened the doors again.It was as if the world was his backyard.A group of paper effigies flew over from somewhere.However, the paper effigy did not self-destruct this time. Instead, it “folded” around Li Changshou’s fake body.The paper effigies turned into parts and pieced together a thousand-feet-tall huge figure in a short period of time.An immortal light flashed past, and the figure concealed the scene of the paper effigy’s hands and feet intertwining inside. It transformed into Li Changshou’s face and Li Changshou’s figure.He was dressed in an ordinary Daoist robe. His long hair was combed and secured with a Dao hoop as he looked at the sky with his hands behind his back.Most of the spiritual energy in the paper effigies’ bodies was used by Li Changshou. It shook the many Great Daos in the place and spread his voice throughout the world.“I’m back. Actually, I’ve never left. There was no need for the various factions to attack. Those who were dissatisfied with the Heavenly Courts were temporarily held back. Now, it’s a battle between Dao Ancestor Hong Jun and me. No one needs to be involved. If I lose again in this battle, all of you will be fine. There’s no need to worry.”This…In the Fire Cloud Cave, the heroic spirit army that was about to set off was stopped by Emperor Huang Xuanyuan with a frown.In the Goddess’s Palace, Goddess Nuwa frowned slightly. The flames that were about to burn in her eyes quickly retreated.The Goddess of Golden Spirit, who was about to give birth soon and was planning to participate in the battle right after giving birth, was also shaken.Youqin Xuanya, who was beside the Goddess of Golden Spirit, had already fainted. She had fainted because she had sensed Youqin Xuanya’s intense emotions.In the Purple Cloud Palace, the Dao Ancestor smiled faintly and raised his hand to point at the Creation Jade Disc in front of him.A bolt of lightning struck the East Continent and shattered the incarnation that Li Changshou had painstakingly condensed. The paper effigies turned into ashes and scattered.The Heavenly Dao trembled. Li Changshou’s voice was cut off and quickly covered up.It was as if there was no previous turmoil in the world.It was like a dream that only a small number of experts saw.However, at that moment, the Heavenly Dao could sense that the Dao Ancestor could sense that the power of the human Dao and living beings imprisoned by the power of the Heavenly Dao were abnormally active.In the Lingxiao Hall of the Heavenly Courts, the Jade Emperor smiled faintly. He lowered his head and looked at the immortals who were mostly puzzled. They actually…He stretched.In front of the main hall of the Lady Mother of the Chariot, Zhao Gongming smiled and was amused. He slapped his legs and wanted to suppress his laughter.Most of the immortals were at a loss. They were wondering why the Dao runes that they had heard and sensed were so familiar.There were also two or three immortals from Jie School with higher cultivation levels. They realized that there was another figure in their Dao hearts.A name was stuck in their throats, but they could not shout or move.“That’s not right.” Zhao Gongming frowned. “Brother, what are you doing? That doesn’t make sense. Could it be that you were discovered and forced to speak? It doesn’t seem like your personality.”The immortals from the Jie School looked at each other and did not know what to say.It was not only Zhao Gongming. At that moment, all the mighty figures of the Primordial World who remembered Li Changshou and captured the scene just now were puzzled. They could not understand what had happened.What happened next?He stood up and said such words to boost morale. He also said that he could let the various factions wait and see. He would fight the Dao Ancestor alone.Shouldn’t it be a grand entrance next?The Dao Ancestor, the Heavenly Dao, the Saints, and the mighty figures began to search the world continuously, hoping to find other clues.In a sense, Li Changshou had already taken the initiative.…On Mount E’Mei, in the forest that was filled with laughter.Yang Jian was playing with the grilled fish in front of him. He looked at Yang Chan’er, who was pouncing on butterflies with a maidservant and could not help but smile.For some reason, the vertical eye on his forehead trembled slightly. It opened automatically and quickly closed.The voices that had just been seized by the Heavenly Dao appeared in Yang Jian’s heart.“I’m back. Actually, I’ve never left…”Who?For some reason, Yang Jian raised his hand and touched his sleeve. He took out a sealed treasure pouch.He had forgotten who had said that to him.“This item is very important. There are no treasures inside. It’s just some words and strategies.”“If one day, I leave this world and the world and the Heavenly Courts encounter a crisis that is difficult to resolve, or if you feel that you have fallen into the despair that I had previously controlled, the seal will dissipate on its own.”“At that time, you can open it and take a look. There’s the brilliant plan that I left for you inside.”Those words seemed to have been said in a secret place, and they suddenly remained in Yang Jian’s memories.However, he could not remember who the person who spoke was. He only remembered that the person’s voice was gentle and made him respect him, but he did not dare to face him.Previously, Yang Jian had seen the brocade pouch a few times, but the seal on it had not been opened.Today, at that moment!The mouth of the brocade pouch suddenly… opened.…In the Kun Peng Mystic Realm, where Li Changshou’s main body was.Black and white Qi flashed in Li Changshou’s eyes. The Connate Cardinal treasure, the Taiji Painting, appeared in front of him and disappeared.In the main control room of the Kun Peng, Great Daoist Master Xuan Du, who was staring at the “route” in front of him, opened his left hand. Black and white Qi condensed in his palm and slowly condensed into a Taiji Painting.That was the “mystical ability” of the supreme treasure Taiji Painting. It ignored all barriers and teleported freely between the “seeds” that it had planted.Of course, it was very difficult to bury such a seed. It had to be an expert who had a deep understanding of the Yin-Yang Great Dao. Only by cooperating with the Taiji Painting could one achieve the goal of crossing the Chaos Sea.Great Daoist Master Xuan Du sensed something and his expression turned solemn.“Senior Brother, the plan is in advance. Everything can be controlled. I’ll delay the Dao Ancestor for a while. I’ll rely on you.”Xuan Du took a deep breath and immediately sent a voice transmission to Kong Xuan. He activated the Taiji Painting.The Yin-Yang Qi completely enveloped the huge Kun Peng Ark. Five-colored Divine Light erupted from the tail of the Kun Peng Ark. The Kun Peng Ark, which was already at its top speed, instantly…It pierced through the chaotic universe in the Chaos Sea!Li Changshou took a deep breath and deduced the last step.At that moment, there were no more possibilities. The derivation tree had reached the end.He had to face this battle head-on.At that moment, he was only using a few insignificant trump cards. He could attract the attention of the world and tell the Goddess of Mercy and the Human Emperor seniors of the Fire Cloud Cave that they did not have to attack. He had already completed most of his expectations.The true trump cards of the Heavenly Fiend technique were the Void Bodhi and the Void Dispelling Great Dao.After thinking about it, he sent a voice transmission to Yun Xiao and Ling’e to enter the hall. He prepared some words in his heart and said when they floated in, “It’s time for the final battle.”Li Changshou said seriously, “I’m going to the Primordial World personally later. I’ll come back after I’m done. You don’t have to worry about my safety. I’m 100% confident that I can escape unscathed. If I can’t do it, I won’t force myself. There are still a few spies of the Dao Ancestor in the Kun Peng Mystic Realm. The other party is not strong enough and has been affected. You can just trap them later. There’s no need to publicize or punish them.”Yun Xiao immediately said, “I can go with you.”Li Changshou was in a dilemma.“Sister.”Ling’e shouted softly. She raised her hand and held Yun Xiao’s hand. She pursed her lips and smiled at Yun Xiao.“We’ll wait for Senior Brother to return. If we follow, we will not be able to participate in the battle between the Dao Ancestor and Senior Brother. Instead, we will be easily captured by the Dao Ancestor when he is desperate. We will leave behind two paper effigies later and escape into the Chaos Sea to avoid the Dao Ancestor’s eyes. That’s the most important thing.”Li Changshou was rendered speechless.Finally, his junior sister had grown up!Yun Xiao still wanted to persist. Ling’e persuaded her gently and gave her senior brother an expression that said, “Don’t worry, I will look after Sister Yun Xiao.”Li Changshou said warmly, “If we don’t have the Taiji Painting to support us from here to the Primordial World, it will take at least decades for Yun Xiao to arrive with her Dharma powers.”Ling’e blinked and the corners of her mouth twitched. She could not help but place a hand on her forehead.

How could I have forgotten about that!?!

Ling’e, who had wanted to form the strongest support group with Sister Yun Xiao after her senior brother left, instantly teared up and looked at Li Changshou pitifully.Li Changshou was defeated and sighed.“Forget it. Call those who want to go together and enter the Primordial Chaos Golden Chalice together. I’ll bring you along. We’ll go anywhere together. Fortunately, I’m well prepared this time.”Yun Xiao pursed her lips and looked at Li Changshou apologetically.Li Changshou naturally knew what she was feeling guilty about. It was just that she felt that she had not helped anything and insisted on causing trouble. She felt a little guilty.However, just as Ling’e had said, no one could participate in the battle with the Dao Ancestor unless they were at the level of the five Saints before the six Saints.Li Changshou waved his hand and asked them to quickly call for help.Then, he closed his eyes and said seriously, “Four Mei Guardians, don’t hold back. It’s the last battle.”The four guardians answered in unison. Their faces were filled with holy light.They firmly believed that they were contributing to saving more living beings. Even if these living beings were completely different from the otherworldly demons, such a matter still had a profound meaning.They had activated the Dao of the Heavenly Fiend to the extreme!In the Primordial World.The five continents.Beside the lotus pond on the spiritual mountain, the “Void Bodhi” opened its eyes and looked at the water surface for a while. It seemed to have something on its mind and sighed softly.He heard a warm voice beside his ear. It was Saint Jie Yin.“Bodhi, don’t be afraid. That person can no longer cause waves. He was just bluffing just now.”Li Changshou could not bear to see that happen.He stood up and bowed to the residence of the Saint. Then, he sighed.Some words were better not said.Some things had to come this far.He whispered, “Teacher, I want to borrow something.”Saint Jie Yin frowned slightly and keenly sensed the faint change in the Void Bodhi’s tone.Light flickered. Saint Jie Yin appeared beside the lotus pond and looked at the Void Bodhi.“What is it?”Li Changshou pointed his finger at the lotus pond. The Golden Cicada inside flew out with a seal. As Li Changshou made a gesture, it turned into an inch long and was easily pinched by him.“Golden Cicada.”The guide raised his eyebrows and stared at his beloved disciple.“You…”Li Changshou lowered his head and bowed. When he stood up again, his grayish-white hair slowly turned black. His body was three inches tall and his shoulders were half an inch wide. His face transformed into Li Changshou’s original appearance.“I’ll use the name of the Void Bodhi to apologize to you, Senior. The Void Bodhi has long died from a mental demon, and I am his mental demon.”The guide’s face became much older in an instant. His eyes were filled with anger. “Aren’t you afraid that I will kill you here?”“Senior, you can’t do that.”Li Changshou shook his head slightly. The Dao runes around him suddenly became empty and mysterious. His body was like a thin fog.“There is no me, no emptiness.”Li Changshou seemed to have disappeared from the world. In the thin fog, a purple-black chain appeared and exploded.The faint voice sounded.“Although all living beings are ultimately nothingness, my Dao heart is constant. Why did it turn illusory? However, the Dao of Time’s projection was illusory. I will not move. I will not move forward. Everything in the world is tangible. The tides will have their own end. What you see is a ‘process’, and there is no ‘end’. I’m here. There’s no free time.”Li Changshou’s figure condensed again, but his Dao Realm rose rapidly.He did not say anything else. He cupped his hands at Saint Jie Yin and pinched the Golden Cicada to leave the spiritual mountain.“Where are you going?”Jie Yin suddenly asked.“In front of the Dao Ancestor of the Purple Cloud Palace.” Li Changshou smiled and said, “I haven’t finished discussing the Dao with him. I still have a match with him. Senior, rest.”Before he finished speaking, Li Changshou disappeared.In the sky above the Aparagodānīya Continent, a meteor rushed into the sky. The three thousand Great Dao trembled.Trump card: Patriarch Bodhi, the Great Dao of Space Dispelling.
