Chapter 464 The Frightening Story of the Primordial World

Streams of light streaked across the void. The group of “pressureers” arrived as promised.The first batch of enemy Qi Refinement cultivators who arrived there were mostly Perfected Immortals and Heaven Immortals. There were about a thousand of them, and two Golden Immortals led the way. Their cultivation levels were not high.It was only a “carefree” power.At that moment, Li Changshou did not understand the rules of the Immortal Dao factions in the Trichiliocosm. However, he knew that the thousands of people were just the appetizer.In the World Edge Mystic Realm, excluding the “аdrifts”…the wielders of the bright eminences’ jade talismans, just the group of old grannies from the World Edge Pavilion would be difficult to obtain without at least ten or twenty Golden Immortals.Moreover, they had an expert like Old Madam Bian.In the Trichiliocosm where supreme-grade Postnatal numinous treasures could be called ‘supreme treasures’, one’s cultivation level was almost equivalent to one’s combat strength.Since Old Madam Bian could support such a huge family business, she could not be underestimated. Through the paper effigies that were currently hiding in various parts of the void, Li Changshou used the same trick again and formed an “immortal senses detection network”. He had long sensed that there were more experts hiding tens of thousands of kilometers away and were slowly approaching the Small World.The other party was also well-versed in the way of “pressure” and “negotiation”. In the rear of the enemy’s army, there were two figures with deep and dense Dao auras. They emitted a perfect aura, as if they were disciples of the Western Saints.Those situations were basically within Li Changshou’s expectations.At that moment, Li Changshou and Lu Yue had stopped talking and were quietly standing at the back of the first batch of experts.They hid in the tiny “mustard-seed universe” and locked onto the enemy vanguard who had negative karma. They had already prepared a large amount of incapacitating agents and poisonous pills.However…“No wonder the Great Tribulation fell.” In a tall building in the World Edge Pavilion, Qin Tianzhu stood on the roof and looked at the streams of light outside the Small World Array. He sighed softly. Li Changshou smiled and said, “The human race reproduces too quickly. The human body is a Connate Dao Body. There will always be people with higher potential. Even though there are limited treasures in a world, the spiritual energy is almost limitless. Now, there is no lack of cultivation techniques. A group of experts could be born in tens of thousands of years. As long as he enters the Golden Immortal realm, he will be able to live forever without suffering any calamities. It’s reasonable for such a situation to happen.”Qin Tianzhu nodded slowly and said, “The number of Golden Immortal realm experts in the Heavenly Courts is inferior to that of a flourishing Sector of the human race.”“Everything has a start.”Li Changshou explained warmly, “Ever since the Heavenly Courts defeated the demons, seven human Golden Immortals have joined us. They are only Heavenly Courts’ Itinerant Immortals. When the Purple Cloud Palace discusses the matter of the Great Tribulation, what the Heavenly Courts have to worry about is no longer the lack of experts. Instead, they have to tighten the gap. They can’t let those who want to avoid the Great Tribulation or those with evil intentions enter the Heavenly Courts to take up positions.”Qin Tianzhu nodded with a smile and sighed.]“When the great calamity descends, I wonder how many lives will be lost. To the Heavenly Dao, what is the difference between living beings and mountain rocks? The Heavenly Dao wants the world to be stable and there would no longer be any disasters from the Dragon-Phoenix Initial Tribulation. In the ancient times, all living beings were cultivated without restraint. Over the countless years, they had accumulated a large number of experts. At the end of the ancient times, there were as many Zenith Heaven Immortals as dogs and Golden Immortals everywhere. It really can’t happen again… The current world is even more fragile than the Primordial World in the ancient times.”Li Changshou pondered and nodded slowly. However, he heard a long sigh behind him.Old Madam Bian, who was sitting at the back, sighed and said, “What General Qin said just now is that the experts of the Heavenly Courts are inferior to those of a Major Chiliocosm. That is indeed wrong. The Heavenly Courts had the Jade Emperor and Queen Mother, who were of the same generation as the Saint. They controlled the operation of the Heavenly Dao and received the protection of the Heavenly Dao. They were also the leaders of the current calamity. They were the living beings of more than a hundred worlds. Together, they were far inferior. In the Primordial World, the path of cultivation had been tough for me. Living beings fought for survival, Qi Refinement cultivators fought for opportunities, and a Golden Immortal rose up, accompanied by countless bloodshed. However, the Golden Immortal realm is only in the realm of cultivation. Longevity and freedom were just jokes. Only mighty figures and great mystical abilities could be a little at ease. There are six Saints above all living beings who can easily destroy the Chiliocosm and refine the wind, fire, water, and earth! What the Golden Immortals can do and what the Dao can do are just different.”If one’s Dao was perfect, it meant that he had attained the Zenith Heaven Dao.Qin Tianzhu smiled and said, “Old Madam is wise.”Li Changshou looked outside the array formation with his hands behind his back. There were more than ten enemy figures pressing forward. They each released their auras, as if they were about to curse…Heavenly Dao, Time, and Heaven and Earth.Saints, universe, and ants.The Art of Equalization existed between all living beings. If there was an imbalance, there would be a great calamity. Li Changshou suppressed his emotions.A gentle greeting came from the sky. “Is the person in charge of the World Edge Pavilion willing to go out for a chat?”“Do we have to be polite before resorting to force?”An old woman snorted coldly with an unfriendly expression.At that moment, the dozen or so people standing or sitting on the roof of the tall building all looked at Li Changshou.Although the person here was only the incarnation of the Heavenly Courts’ Water God and was only a paper effigy at the late-stage of the Heaven Immortal realm, he had only been there for four to five days. However, for some reason…He became the main pillar.Li Changshou crushed the jade token in his hand. In the Small World of the World Edge Mystic Realm, dozens of streams of light flew into the sky.Three old women in the Golden Immortal realm flew out. The rest were at the late-stage of the Heaven Immortal realm.Most of them were women. They were dressed in armor and holding ordinary numinous treasures. They looked at the “guests” with unfriendly expressions.The two Golden Immortals’ eyes were filled with coldness.“Is your pavilion unwilling to appear and talk to even a Deputy Pavilion Master?”An old woman said, “You can’t compare to the former army. Why do you want to see my Deputy Pavilion Master? Let the people behind you come faster! When your in-charge appears, my in-charge will appear!” Upon hearing that, a Golden Immortal elder could not help but frown.“The World Edge Pavilion really has the confidence to take on today’s battle? Don’t seek death.”He seemed to have some feelings for the World Edge PavilionThe people from the World Edge Pavilion did not reply. One of them held a jade token and opened a crack in the array formation behind him.The sounds of immortal music and laughter could be heard from afar. The scene of dancing and shadows could be seen everywhere. Everything in the mystic realm was normal. They were not disturbed by the commotion outside at all.That was the best answer.The dozen or so immortals who had come to call for the battle did not say anything else. They could not figure out the background of the World Edge Pavilion.Behind them, the “army” that was originally moving forward slowly began to accelerate. A river of light appeared in the void, and thousands of streams of light flew over.Among them, there was a small group of experts that the Western Sect had temporarily transferred over.In the Small Mustard universe, Li Changshou’s Golden Immortal paper effigy and Lu Yue looked at each other and smiled.Lu Yue sent a voice transmission. “The other party did not use any ordinary immortal weapons. We can go all out today.”Li Changshou also said, “They should be rushing all the way. If they use the immortal army, it will probably be difficult for them to rush here in three days.”“Changgeng, shall we… follow the plan?”“This is the first battle between Heaven and the Primordial World. We have to scare them.”“Hahaha! I can’t wait anymore! I’m really happy to fight alongside you, Junior Brother!”The two of them whispered to each other. Then, they stopped and hid quietly.An hour later, more streams of light arrived outside the World Edge Mystic Realm’s array formation. They were arranged in a loose formation.Seventeen to eighteen Golden Immortal experts stood in a line. The men, women, old, and young looked different. Their auras were mixed with some negative karma.Of course, in the eyes of a disciple of a Saint, it was only an average 11.5% power of “carefree”.It was a coincidence. The two experts who were suspected to be the disciples of the Saints of the Western Sect happened to stop in the middle of the enemy immortal battle formation, leaving the place where Li Changshou and Lu Yue were hiding. However…Five feet.Lu Yue was about to attack, but Li Changshou raised his hand in time and grabbed Lu Yue’s arm. He shook his head with a solemn expression.“Senior Brother, there’s no hurry.” “I’m anxious. I’m anxious.” Lu Yue smiled and said, “Let’s follow the plan.”At that moment, the two of them continued to hide in a sinister manner. They began to emit wisps of faint poisonous medicinal pills…A small amount makes a lot, and warm water makes an immortal.Although the two enemy experts were hidden in the fog, they could not hide from Li Changshou and Lu Yue’s detection at such a close distance.It was an old man and a young Daoist. The pressure on the young Daoist was stronger. It was not like any disciple of the Western Saints that Li Changshou had seen before.However, Li Changshou sensed an aura similar to that of Ksitigarbha on the young Daoist. The cultivation techniques that the two of them cultivated should be similar.Li Changshou secretly reminded Lu Yue that if he could only leave one person behind later, he would kill the old Daoist priest and leave the young Daoist priest alive so that he could bring the news back.Naturally, it would be best to kill them all.Lu Yue was about to agree and say a few confident and arrogant words when he heard a conversation from the front.The two experts muttered…The old Daoist priest was puzzled. “Dao Brother, why do I feel like someone is staring at us?”The young Daoist smiled confidently. “It’s fine. Now that the great calamity has arrived, most of the experts of the Dao Sect don’t dare to move around recklessly. They should be experts who are enjoying themselves in the World Edge Mystic Realm. We are only here to scare the World Edge Pavilion this time. Those experts have no reason to go against us. At that moment, the Water God’s incarnation is in the World Edge Pavilion. As long as we taunt him today and force the World Edge Pavilion to lower its head, it will be considered a success. There’s no need to kill and fight.”“The Water God is not easy to deal with.”“That’s right. We cannot be careless. Later, we will stand here and shout at them from afar. What can an incarnation of his do to me?” “Wonderful.”The old Daoist priest praised. The difference in their statuses was obvious.At that moment, the old Daoist priest snorted coldly. His voice sounded in the fog, accompanied by the trembling of the Great Dao and suppressing the entire World Edge Mystic Realm.The walking stick in Old Madam Bian’s hand trembled slightly. Layers of array formations were set up in the various pavilions and halls.However, at that moment, he would have to spend countless Spirit Stones…However, for the sake of the customers’ experience, the World Edge Pavilion was going all out.The old lady released her own pressure and collided with the old Daoist priest’s pressure. She also snorted coldly.The old Daoist priest in the sky chided coldly, “Since I’m here, why don’t you come out and meet me, Pavilion Master Tianya?”The moment he spoke, it was the style of the Western Sect.On the high building in the World Edge Mystic Realm, Bian Zhuang, who had changed into silver armor, held the nine-toothed rake and was about to go out with the two old women and Deputy Pavilion Lords.Li Changshou smiled and said warmly, “Bian Zhuang, come.”“Okay!”Bian Zhuang quickly walked to Li Changshou and bowed with the Nine-Toothed Harrow.Li Changshou extended his right hand. Wisps of Mysterious Yellow aura condensed into the image of the Mysterious Yellow Pagoda in his palm. He placed the image into Bian Zhuang’s chest and patted his shoulder. “Relax. Don’t forget your words. If you forget your words, mock at will.” “Yes! Lord Water God, don’t worry. I understand!”Bian Zhuang grinned and cupped his fists again. He looked up at the outside of the small world and leaped into the sky with the Nine-Toothed Harrow.Qin Tianzhu and Li Changshou looked at each other and smiled. They were waiting for the show to start.As Bian Zhuang flew out of the array formation, the two old women behind him immediately raised their auras to the maximum. They protected him and allowed him to fly to the front of the array formation.Bian Zhuang casually pointed out a white cloud and… sat down.He crossed his legs and pulled out an embroidered handkerchief with the fragrance of a young girl from his armor. He wiped his hands and turned a blind eye to the 5,000 to 6,000 figures and the pressure that filled the sky.The old Daoist priest hiding in the fog said calmly, “Could the Pavilion Master of the World Edge Pavilion be such a rude and frivolous person?” Bian Zhuang ignored him and put away the embroidered handkerchief. The Nine-Toothed Harrow beside him quickly shrank to three inches long. He picked it up and combed its fringe.Behind him, two fairies at the Heaven Immortal realm walked forward with tea. Bian Zhuang took it and took a sip. After gulping it a few times, he turned around and spat it into the teacup that another fairy was holding.Bian Zhuang said slowly, “Who was shouting just now?”In the fog, the old Daoist priest’s eyes were filled with killing intent. The pressure from his body swept towards Bian Zhuang and was barely blocked by the two old women behind Bian Zhuang. The old Daoist priest cursed, “Don’t seek your own death.”“Heh.” Bian Zhuang smiled in disdain. “Do you know who you are threatening? He was originally a fifth-stage Immortal God of the Heavenly Courts, the Deputy Commander of the Heavenly River Navy. He had worshiped the Jade Emperor in the Lingxiao Hall, and the Tushita Palace had received treasures from the Great Daoist Master. He had heard the Water God’s admonishment in the Water God Residence. You dare to bark at the so-called experts of the Heavenly Courts just because you have some cultivation level. There are countless demons in the past. Now, hehe, I’m afraid there are not many.”“Heh, is the World Edge Pavilion really not afraid of death?”The old Daoist priest said calmly, “You’re just a small Immortal God of the Heavenly Courts. So what if I kill you here?”“Really?”Bian Zhuang recalled the lines given by the Water God and the sentence, “No matter what the other party says, just continue according to the script. There’s no need to talk to him.”However, at that moment, Bian Zhuang felt that the lines given by the Water God could be used there.Bian Zhuang placed the Nine-Toothed Harrow in his palm and sized it up carefully. He said, “You probably don’t know what treasure this is. That’s right. Not everyone can see a treasure refined by Grand Supreme Elder.”As soon as he said that, everyone looked at Bian Zhuang’s palm.It was not just the Qi Refinement cultivators in front of the array formation. Even the guests in the World Edge Mystic Realm were paying attention to that matter. They were staring at the Nine-Toothed Harrow in Bian Zhuang’s hand…There was indeed a mysterious and obscure Dao rune.Bian Zhuang smiled and said, “You guys don’t know what’s good for you. Since you guys are here today, I have to teach you a lesson. I just have to flick my harrow gently.”Ding ~Bian Zhuang’s fingers gently tapped on the silver teeth of the rake, producing a crisp sound.“In the place covered by my immortal senses, the precious harrow will randomly choose someone who is hostile to me and ignite the negative karma in his body.”As soon as he finished speaking, it was quiet outside the array. It was so quiet that one could hear a pin drop.Someone laughed first. Then, loud laughter came from everywhere.Suddenly, amidst the laughter, a middle-aged man among the more than ten Golden Immortals in the row suddenly widened his eyes. Black blood seeped out of his eyes, mouth, and nostrils!He raised his hand and grabbed his throat. His body fell forward weakly. His essence soul was like a melted wax statue. In an instant, his entire body turned into a pool of black water that scattered in the sky… The world was peaceful again. Bian Zhuang’s eyelids twitched slightly. He forced himself to calm down and continued to follow the script.Then, Bian Zhuang flicked the rake in his hand twice and continued calmly and dryly, “I wonder if the Great Daoist Master will blame me for using this treasure in front of my house. Ah, there’s no choice. There are not many experts in the Heavenly Courts. I can only rely on some treasures to protect myself.”“Ah!”“Save me!” Before Bian Zhuang could finish speaking, two more screams were heard. A man and a woman fell down. It was similar to the previous person.Three pools of black water, three longevity Golden Immortals!Everything happened in an instant. It happened so naturally that it was extremely strange!The other party was in chaos! The three Golden Immortals from different worlds melted just like that…The old Daoist priest in the fog made a prompt decision and immediately used his mystical ability to take down Bian Zhuang!Bian Zhuang’s gaze froze. His fingers slid across the nine silver teeth of the miniature Nine-Toothed Harrow, causing a soft sound.The old Daoist priest, who was about to charge forward, was shocked. He hurriedly looked inside himself and stared at the black aura that had invaded his essence soul!He did not notice anything!“Poison! Someone poisoned me!”The old Daoist priest roared and immediately suppressed the toxicity. He turned around and was about to escape. However, just as his immortal power surged, his vision turned black. The wisp of black Qi in his essence soul turned into dozens of colorful lights!As the angle of attack was too good, there was too much time, and the opportunity was too rare, Lu Yue relied on Li Changshou’s guidance to test more than ten types of Great Dao Poison Pills on the old Daoist priest. The effect was obviously good. The old Daoist priest hurriedly raised his hand to grab the young Daoist priest beside him. However, he realized that the young Daoist priest’s face was pale. He fell into the fog and trembled!The sky of the Small World suddenly darkened.The thousands of figures fell into chaos. However, the chaos only lasted for a moment. Thousands of Perfected Immortals collapsed at the same time. The one to two hundred Heaven Immortals did not struggle at all. Their essence souls were dispirited and they fell unconscious.That was Li Changshou’s masterpiece. More than twenty paper effigies had been set up everywhere to release poison at the same time.The incapacitating agent was naturally created by Lu Yue. It was colorless, odorless, and did not have much power. The dozen-odd Golden Immortals reacted the fastest. When they saw that something was amiss, they immediately wanted to escape. At that moment, their essence souls melted and turned into a pool of black water.It was Lu Yue who had used the Great Dao poison that he was most proud of.The small world was peaceful.On the tall building, Old Madam Bian of the World Edge Pavilion and the other old women looked shocked as they watched Li Changshou and Qin Tianzhu’s backs.In the various pavilions of joy, there were guests whose Dao hearts had been shaken. Some of them frowned, while others… might need the Ambition Pill and Poisonous Dragon Wine to intervene and treat them in time.The dozens of experts from the World Edge Pavilion in the sky suffered the greatest impact. The Golden Immortals’ faces were pale, and the dozens of Heaven Immortals’ Dao hearts trembled.They had worked hard to cultivate for so many years. In an instant, they turned into a pool of black water…Bian Zhuang’s Adam’s apple trembled a few times. He sighed softly. “Why must you force me?”In the fog, the old Daoist priest spat out a mouthful of blood and cursed softly, “Who are you!?!”“Deputy Commander of the Heavenly River Navy.” Bian Zhuang smiled and put away the Nine-Toothed Harrow. He calmly said the last line. “By the way, he’s also the Young Pavilion Master of this World Edge Pavilion. Aren’t you going to escape?”At that moment, the two old women behind Bian Zhuang immediately wanted to move forward. The young Daoist suddenly shone with golden light and a string of prayer beads appeared above his head.The young Daoist opened his eyes and used a treasure to suppress his severely injured essence soul. He grabbed the old Daoist priest and was about to leave.At this moment! A palm appeared out of thin air. Black wind surged in the palm and hit the young Daoist priest’s chest at lightning speed. Waves of black gas almost destroyed the young Daoist priest’s essence soul.He would cripple his Dao base and sever his Dao!“How dare you!”The young Daoist gritted his teeth and shouted angrily. However, he had already suffered the blow. His body turned into a ball of sanguine light and seemed to have used some kind of escape technique. With a whoosh, he disappeared into the void.Just as he struck out with his palm, a beam of light flickered beside him. The old Daoist priest’s head was sent flying and smashed down. He did not resist at all…The void finally quietened down.It was strange and peaceful. Li Changshou’s Golden Immortal realm paper effigy put his sword back into the scabbard on his back. Lu Yue stood beside him with his hands behind his back, looking regretful. Since he was acting, he naturally had to put on a full act.Li Changshou said loudly, “General Bian, are you frightened?” “Hahaha.” Bian Zhuang laughed loudly, but his laughter was a little pale. “Thank you.”“In that case, we’ll take our leave for now. General Bian, you can appear if you need anything.” After saying that, Li Changshou’s Golden Immortal paper effigy and Lu Yue turned into two streams of light and flew into the void. In the blink of an eye, they disappeared.Bian Zhuang felt his mouth go dry and he was speechless.On the tall building, Li Changshou turned around and looked at Old Madam Bian. The corners of his lips curled into a smile. “The World Edge Pavilion will deal with these enemies.”Old Lady Bian’s eyes flickered as she smiled and sighed.“The Water God is really powerful. He can destroy a faction with a snap of his fingers. Men, go and kill all the intruders. There’s no need to leave anyone alive. Post a notice to the outside world that the World Edge Pavilion will open the door to welcome guests. However, not everyone can cause trouble. Let’s spread the news that my great grandson is only working in the Heavenly Courts. Let some major factions not think too much about it.”The old women and women behind him lowered their heads and agreed.Qin Tianzhu carefully pondered for a while before understanding most of the meaning behind the old lady’s words.Just as he was about to make some interesting comments, he realized that the Water God had already started to talk to Old Lady Bian about brewing tea. It seemed that nothing had happened.This…Qin Tianzhu looked a little emotional.He should just do the simple task given by the Jade Emperor. Changgeng’s world was not suitable for him.
