Chapter 420 How to Fight This Battle!

This…Why did they attack without any preparation? In front of the mountain gate, Li Changshou rode a cloud at an unprecedented speed. He rushed over from Little Qiong Peak and cupped his hands at Young Master Qiao, who was outside the mountain gate. He smiled and said, “Young Master, how can you be free?”The charming young man smiled and said warmly, “Did I come too suddenly?”“Why so?”Li Changshou smiled and made an inviting gesture. Then, he looked at the old immortal guarding the sect and bowed.“This Young Master Xiao is a good friend of mine. I wonder if you can bring her into the sect first. I will go to the Hundred Mortal Hall to obtain an entry token later.”The elder who was on duty to guard the sect today was in a dilemma. “Although I trust you, Disciple-Nephew Changshou, the sect rules are like this. You cannot bring anyone in without permission…”Good job! The elders did their best! In that case, based on his gentle personality, he would not make things difficult for him. Indeed, the “delicate young master” was already prepared to speak and invite Li Changshou to take a walk around the sect. There was no need to enter.However!Whoosh…There was a loud sound. A ray of green light flew over from the Heaven-Breaking Peak and turned into a wooden sign that landed in front of Li Changshou.Emptiness, ahem, the Sect Leader Ji Wuyou!Ji Wuyou’s voice sounded from the wooden sign.“A few days ago, you helped the sect solve the problem of the Five-Element Array Formation’s spiritual energy being continuously transformed. This has troubled me for tens of thousands of years. I will reward you with this token. With this token, you can bring people in and out of the sect and the Immortal Du Hall at will.” Li Changshou squinted and smiled. He took the wooden sign and said, “Thank you for your kindness, Sect Leader.”The elders of the Immortal Dao Sect who were guarding the sect gate hurriedly let them in. The elders were still puzzled. They wondered, ‘Why did he have to use the Sect Leader Token that they usually did not see in a hundred years for such a trivial matter?’Couldn’t the Sect Leader just send a voice transmission?The “delicate young master” blinked. Clearly, she did not expect such a situation. She stood on the cloud and watched with a smile. When Li Changshou invited her in, she rode the cloud forward and jumped onto the white cloud under Li Changshou’s feet.She whispered, “Did I disturb you?”Li Changshou said warmly, “You suddenly came over. I was just a little flustered. Can you say that I was disturbing you?” At that moment, Li Changshou rode a cloud and brought the “delicate young master” towards the Little Qiong Peak. In the small hut beside the Immortal Du Hall, Sect Leader Ji Wuyou squinted and smiled.Although he did not know who was looking for Changshou, he knew that it was definitely a high official from the three sects. How could he make the guests unhappy because of the rules of the Immortal Du Sect?He had to respect the Little Daoist Master!This time, my path is wider!When he landed at the Little Qiong Peak, the “delicate young master” sensed the layers of array formations around the Little Qiong Peak and immediately put away the illusion technique in relief. As the faint fog dissipated, she returned to her original appearance. Her eyes were watery and she pressed her thin lips together, staying silent for a long time.The clouds made the fog condense into sleeves. Which immortal would have cold branches? Who else could it be but Fairy Yun Xiao? Li Changshou brought Fairy Yun Xiao to the spiritual lake of the Little Qiong Peak. Yun Xiao was calm and did not think much about it. She only looked around curiously. The fairy asked, “Did you get injured when you fought with the demon prince?”“No.” Li Changshou smiled and said, “It’s just a little too much of my immortal power has been consumed. I wasn’t actually fighting with him. I was just using the treasures that Teacher gave me to compete with him.”Yun Xiao continued, “I’m here today because I have two things that I’m worried about. I’ll give you some instructions. Is the one on the neighboring peak the ancient demon general, Bai Ze? Can I go and make him feel more afraid?”“No, no. Mr. Bai is currently depressed.” Li Changshou smiled and said, “For now… I should treat him better. He was tortured by the Great Daoist Master and me previously.”“Tortured?”Yun Xiao blinked and asked curiously, “Did you use some kind of torture method?”Li Changshou led Yun Xiao to land under the willow tree. “It’s a long story. I’ll tell you slowly later. Since you’re here, greet my master and junior sister first.” “Yes.” Yun Xiao nodded. She did not have the shyness of a young girl. She was not nervous or embarrassed at all. Even if she went to the Grand Pure One to greet him, her Dao heart would be clear and flawless, let alone on the Little Qiong Peak.Li Changshou led Yun Xiao to a few straw huts and sent a voice transmission to them.Ling’e was the first to push open the window of her straw hut. A small head popped out. When she saw Yun Xiao’s figure, she was clearly stunned. She then retracted her head. Ling’e: !!! W-What’s going on!?!How could that be!?!Fairy Yun Xiao is here? Finally, they had reached the scene of the house being lost, the city gate being set on fire, and their home being stolen! Ling’e pursed her lips and hurriedly sat in front of the dressing table. Her eyes burned with a fighting spirit, but at that moment, she felt like she had been doused with cold water…Yun Xiao, that’s Fairy Yun Xiao! The experts of the three sects recorded in the ancient books, the few experts below the Saint in the world, were instantly charmed after just taking a glance of her extraordinary, and peerless beauty… That was not something that could be matched by a pretty face or an exquisite and well-proportioned figure. Her temperament was similar to the Dao and was not something ordinary that others could not imitate even if they wanted to!Dong! Ling’e sat in front of the dressing table with her legs crossed. Her eyes were listless, and her breathing stopped. She seemed to have a glitch.Lan Ling’e, are you going to give Senior Brother up just like that?This is your senior brother whom you have long decided to follow for life!At that moment, there were sounds of laughter coming from the straw hut next door… “Master, Fairy Yun is my good friend. I mentioned her to you before. She came to our sect to visit.”“Oh, right, right. Changshou has mentioned you many times! Fairy Yun please make yourself at home! Ah, my Changshou is finally a mature immortal. Previously, I was most worried that he would not be able to make any good friends. I was really worried. Hahahahahaha…”Ling’e frowned and pursed her lips. She clenched her fists and cried. Her master had betrayed her so quickly! He said that he would unconditionally support her and Li Changshou, his personal disciples, to become Dao companions! Ling’e took a deep breath and her Dao heart burned with a fighting spirit. Her mother was right.If a woman was not ruthless, her family would not be stable. What she wanted to fight for was the position in her senior brother’s heart. She also had the advantage of being close to him for so many years. At this moment, she only needed to stabilize her defense and take every step carefully.She would not necessarily lose to the famous Fairy Yun Xiao! Isn’t she just the disciple of the Saint who has an extraordinary cultivation level? She was still the heavens! “Is this your junior sister?” A gentle voice suddenly sounded beside her ear. Ling’e suddenly felt a cool breeze brushing past her ear. She no longer had so many messy thoughts and her Dao heart became much calmer.She turned around and saw Li Changshou standing with his hands behind his back in front of the straw hut.The fairy beside him smiled and looked at her.At that moment, the door frame seemed to have disappeared. Her senior brother also disappeared. Only the fairy’s slightly apologetic gaze and gentle smile remained. It was as if she was saying to her, “Sorry for disturbing you.” She did not have any hostility… Li Changshou said warmly, “Ling’e, don’t be rude.” “Yes.” Ling’e hurriedly stood up and bowed to Yun Xiao. “Greetings, Senior… Yun Xiao.”Yun Xiao took two steps forward and said gently, “There’s no need to be particular about the seniority of the Dao Sect between us. I’ve wasted a lot of your time. Just call me Sister like my two sisters do.” When she finished speaking, Yun Xiao hurriedly said, “Don’t think too much. The reason why I want you to call me Sister is because of my age.” “Sister…”Ling’e blushed and shouted softly. White smoke appeared on her forehead again.Li Changshou looked at Yun Xiao and then at his junior sister. He suddenly realized that Fairy Yun Xiao…You know quite a lot.Clearly, he had paid attention to marriage in the mortal world! “Ling’e, change your clothes first,” Li Changshou said. “I’ll bring you around the peak.” “Yes, Senior Brother.” Ling’e agreed obediently. She could sense the intimacy in Yun Xiao’s eyes and felt a little emotional.After Li Changshou closed the wooden door and went to the lake with Fairy Yun Xiao, Ling’e heaved a long sigh of relief. She sat there and immediately got busy.How could they fight this battle? A woman like Fairy Yun Xiao, who was as gentle as water, would be tempted when she saw her! With Senior Brother’s troublesome personality, even if he did not want to have a Dao companion relationship with Fairy Yun Xiao in the beginning, he would definitely not reject such a powerful person. As time passed, he would naturally develop feelings for her…In the beginning, when other women appeared beside her senior brother, Ling’e felt threatened, but she also saw hope.At that time, Ling’e felt that she could not handle her senior brother’s personality alone. It would be best if someone could help him open his heart… At that time, Ling’e had treated Aunt-Master Jiu Jiu and Senior Sister Youqin as her opponents and allies. However, Aunt-Master Jiu Jiu was too playful.Her good impression of her senior brother was also transferred to the wine. She was the first to show her good impression of her senior brother. In the end, she entered the retirement group of the Little Qiong Peak.As for Senior Sister Youqin, Senior Brother had always felt that she was not mature enough. Moreover, their views were very different. In the end, they walked further and further down the path of Dharma treasures.At that moment, a fairy who perfectly met Li Changshou’s criteria for a Dao companion appeared in the world outside the Little Qiong Peak.After she became an immortal and ascended, Li Changshou opened his heart to her…“Ah.” Ling’e looked at the sideburns that she had combed when she was distracted. After thinking about it, Ling’e took out a jade hairpin from her storage Dharma treasure and stuck it into her hair. She looked at the mirror and looked around.I can’t admit defeat so easily. At most, I’ll cry in front of Senior Brother tonight! Yes, I’ll pick out a beautiful dress first…Li Changshou smiled and introduced the various spiritual fish in the lake and their taste. Fairy Yun Xiao was quite engrossed. When she heard that Li Changshou was going to personally cook a meal later, there was a hint of anticipation in her eyes.After chatting for a while, Li Changshou shifted the topic to serious matters. “Fairy, what are the two things that you mentioned?”Yun Xiao smiled and said, “You are the brainy one in the Dao Sect. Why don’t you guess?”.Li Changshou shook his head and said seriously, “There are no benefits. I can’t be bothered to waste my energy.”“Then, what benefits do you want?”“Um…”Li Changshou pondered for a while. Although he really wanted to say something like a pillow, his skin was not that thick.A moderate amount of restraint was an outstanding quality that all human disciples should have.Li Changshou sighed and said, “Forget it. I still don’t dare to tease you.”Yun Xiao looked at the sparkling lake at the side and said softly, “I won’t hit you with the Golden Chalice.” “Forget it if we fight. We still have to use the Primordial Chaos Golden Chalice!”Li Changshou deliberately frowned. Yun Xiao pursed her lips and chuckled, her eyes filled with reproach. “Stop joking,” Li Changshou said seriously. “You’re here because of Bai Ze and Lu Ya. You’re worried that I’ve been schemed against by the demons, right?” “Yes.” Yun Xiao nodded slightly. “Can Bai Ze be trusted?”Li Changshou said, “I’m still observing. However, Mr. Bai has already obtained 30% of my trust.”“He was the Demon Commander of the past. He was also an extremely intelligent person who had fought with the Demon Master Kun Peng. His mystical ability that allows him to detect calamities is also quite powerful,” Yun Xiao said softly. “He will definitely not join you for no reason.”“I’ll tell you slowly. It’s a long story.” Li Changshou placed his hands behind his back and denounced Lu Ya to the heavens. He talked about how he and his senior brother had captured Bai Ze and how Bai Ze had given him the theory of seeking luck and avoiding danger.Yun Xiao deduced carefully and thought about it carefully. “In that case, it’s 70% to 80% credible.” Li Changshou smiled and said, “Be it because Bai Ze wanted to enter Ren School to avoid the calamity or because he had a premonition that there would be a great calamity in the world, his essence soul is being restrained by Senior Brother Xuan Du. For this auspicious beast, Bai Ze, we cannot use the human mindset to judge his relationship with the demons. Bai Ze might be on a higher level. I can sense that he has a sense of pity for all living beings. Perhaps that is why he is listed as an auspicious beast by the Heavenly Dao.”“Since you understand, I won’t worry anymore,” Yun Xiao said gently. “From what you said just now, I feel that Bai Ze is quite pitiful after being tortured by you.” Li Changshou shrugged and said seriously, “I don’t think I have the ability to devise strategies. If I don’t take precautions, how can I survive until I can stand shoulder to shoulder with you?” Yun Xiao tilted her head slightly and looked at Li Changshou’s face. She said seriously, “Aren’t we walking side by side now?”“Well… It’s just a little arrogance that men often have. Don’t worry about it.” Li Changshou made an inviting gesture. “Firstly, I’m worried that Bai Ze has schemed against me. Secondly, is it because Lu Ya has some powerful Dharma treasures left behind by the Demon Court?”“Huh? What kind of powerful Dharma treasure?”Yun Xiao shook her head gently and said, “I heard that you fought with the prince of the demon race. I was worried that you would put up a strong front for the sake of Ren School when you were injured. I came over to take a look to calm down.” “You and I have really entered the Budding Romance realm.” Li Changshou’s eyes showed that he was slightly moved.Yun Xiao changed the topic. “At the same time, I want to see if you’re as you say. Third Sister often says that human men are most sentimental and there are very few who can be trusted. This is the effect of the Dao of reproduction. I’m relieved to see Ling’e.”Li Changshou was speechless. He called out softly, “Yun Xiao.”“Huh?”Yun Xiao was a little stunned when she saw Li Changshou’s serious expression. Li Changshou sighed and said, “This is not something you should think about. Otherwise, I would feel guilty. Did my mortal heart stain your pure Dao heart?” “Did it?”Fairy Yun Xiao blinked and looked a little uneasy. She said softly, “These are the Great Dao. My Dao heart is not untainted by the mortal world. Otherwise…”Li Changshou calmly retracted his hands and sighed softly.“I won’t wash my hands!” Yun Xiao finally reacted and blushed. “Hmph! You’re indeed very naughty! I’ll go back first!”“I’m just joking. Since we’re already here, we have to have a meal before we leave.” Li Changshou hurriedly said, “Also, Fairy, what do you think about the arrangements of the Little Qiong Peak?” “Huh? I didn’t take a closer look… Nine Heaven Life-soil? Five-Element Sky Change Array? Chaotic Lightning Fiend Array? This…”Yun Xiao frowned and looked at Li Changshou’s smug expression. She could not help but support her forehead with her slender fingers. “Are you sure? There are many more array formations on this peak than on my Three-Immortal Island. What are you afraid of? If there are any powerful enemies that you can’t deal with, I can help you.”Li Changshou shook his head and smiled. “I’m just being prepared.” He paused and turned to look at the straw hut. Ling’e had already arrived on a cloud. Li Changshou said, “If it weren’t for the arrangements here, my main body would have had no choice but to be distracted by the matters at home.”At home…Yun Xiao understood and said gently, “Ling’e is indeed very cute. If she’s free, I really want to bring her to the Three-Immortal Island to stay for a while.” Li Changshou was confused when he heard that. He could not understand the meaning behind those words.There seemed to be a psychological game going on, and the levels were increasing…
