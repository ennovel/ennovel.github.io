Chapter 324: Dad, I Am Back. Battle of You City

“Gou’zi, did you give it pig feed? How did it grow so fat?”Lin Fan recalled that when he left, the nine-headed bug was still the size of a dog. How did it turn from a small water gun into a giant tank after only such a short period of time?The change was just too huge and he nearly couldn’t recognize it.Gou’zi scratched his head and said helplessly, “Young Master, I really didn’t feed it any pig feed. I am actually leaving soon.”Gou’zi didn’t expect Young Master to leave right after returning. What had happened? He felt like he was useless; apart from cleaning and cooking, he didn’t know how to do anything else.In the past, he wanted to work hard and cultivate.But he noticed that he really wasn’t cut out for it; he had no talent at all.“Young Master, where are you going?” Gou’zi asked.Lin Fan said, “I have something to do and will only return after a period of time. There was no need for him to be too anxious.“Cough cough!”At that point, Feng Poliu walked over from afar. When he saw Lin Fan, he coughed a few times like he had something to say.At the side of the cliff.“You headed to the border?” Feng Poliu asked.
