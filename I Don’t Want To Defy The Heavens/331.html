Chapter 331: This Way Is A Bit Wild

Lin Wanyi looked at the unfilial kid who left and smiled happily. However, the smile disappeared right away.Shortly after, he was really solemn and also a little worried.This unfilial child had gone overboard.It seemed really fun but the Alliance had really remembered Fan’er and one of the strongest generals in history. Zhu Daoshen had remembered him in his heart.It was probably really tough for him to live a peaceful life.“Old Master, Young Master is really outstanding. He caused many problems for the Alliance today.” Old Wu said.“Outstanding, but he nearly killed himself. He didn’t do anything properly and just spent his time doing such evil things. He should just cultivate properly.” Lin Wanyi was unhappy.Old Wu smiled.Young Master was right; Old Master really didn’t say the truth. But there was nothing they could do as that was his character.Moreover, he realized that Young Master’s cultivation had become stronger.Although he didn’t know about the exact situation, the feeling Young Master gave him was that he was much stronger than before.….At the Alliance main camp.When Zhu Daoshen and the others returned, the stench from the scene was absolutely disgusting. One could enter and not touch anything but after coming out they would smell like they had fallen into a pile of sh*t.It was really chaotic.Many Alliance members were crying out and on the verge of tears like they had been wronged.Zhu Daoshen’s face was cold and questioned an eight-star general, “Didn’t I ask you all to clear the place? What is going on?”“General, you really can’t blame us. They have already broken down and aren’t listening to orders.” The eight-star general’s face was really bitter and he wanted to cry. His nose was blocked and apart from the smell, he couldn’t smell anything else.It was too tragic.If he hadn’t personally witnessed it, he definitely wouldn’t have believed it.“Drag these people away and find those who are still calm to clean this up. Go.” Zhu Daoshen said. He didn’t expect that they couldn’t even handle such a simple matter. This was the reason why these people would never get promoted.“Yes.” He received the orders and immediately instructed the subordinates. If they didn’t start soon and the general was furious, the outcome would be catastrophic.Some determined Alliance members became the main labor force. They didn’t care about the dirt on their bodies and started to clear the scenes. At the same time, they received orders to remove those who were crying and screaming at the center of the square.Two normal Alliance members looked at one another and at those who should have been the technical members. Without saying a word, they grabbed his legs and dragged him out.“Who am I?”“Where am I?”“This is a sh*t ocean, save me.”That technical member had gone mad and said words that others couldn’t understand. Who knew what he was thinking but he felt really weak and fragile?Those two normal members were expressionless, they didn’t fear anything and were even slightly happy.In the past when they met these technical research members, it was like they were low class.But now they were dragging them like dogs.They felt delighted deep down.“Were you hit by the sh*t?”“Eh yes, it was just a small drizzle. When I was young and played with the kids, I ran too quickly and fell into the sh*t hole. I was drowned up to my nose and if my mom hadn’t save me, I would have died.”“Then, I am slightly better than you, but my family was poor, so I followed my dad and collected all these in exchange for money. That was why I was able to graduate and why I feel indifferent towards the sh*t.”The two of them looked at one another and didn’t expect that they each had such experiences. They instantly felt much closer to one another.The general saw these two who were so diligent and felt much happier. Compared to the others, these two were much better.“Don’t you two find this disgusting?” The general appeared in front of them and asked.The two of them saw the general and were so emotional that they didn’t know what to say.They looked at one another and said in unison, “It is disgusting.”“Then, why didn’t you all object? ” The general asked.The two of them said respectfully, “Everything is for the Alliance.”That reply was perfect. The two of them also didn’t speak about their experiences with sh*t and instead said such a righteous answer.The general was satisfied, “Good, once this ends, you two come report to me.”“Yes general.” They didn’t expect that there would be a day when they would rise up through the ranks.General Gang Xiong’s face was cold and he returned to his own room to bathe. The hot water was unable to wash off the stench. He poured an entire bottle of shampoo and rubbed hard. But even after he washed them all off he still felt like there was still a smell.Pa!Gang Xiong smacked the wall. He didn’t use too much strength, otherwise, the room would collapse.His eyes were red and all his veins popped up.“Kid, one day I will kill you.”Whoever that said that men wouldn’t cry would only say that because they weren’t extremely sad.Now when one was sad, he teared up.Rage Points +999Zhu Daoshen stood with his arms behind his back and his face was really cold. The situation outside was really complicated and it would be really tough to clean the main camp.This matter also had a huge effect on those members.“General, the Alliance Headquarters are looking for you.”“Eh.” Zhu Daoshen nodded his head. He had to report the situation and at the same time, the appearance of Lin Wanyi’s son meant that things had changed.The next day.Maigu City.Pata!Mao Shentai’s expression was extremely ugly, “Damn, Monster Emperor this bastard.”“What?” General Xue Du frowned, “Did Monster Emperor go against his word?”“No.” Mao Shentai told Xue Du what the headquarters told him, “Take a look. Doesn’t this person look familiar? Monster Emperor has messed up our chance.”Xue Du looked at the message. His originally calm expression changed.“It’s him.”He recognized him.“Right, it is him. He is Lin Wanyi’s son. If we capture him, then Lin Wanyi will be there for the killing.” Mao Shentai really regretted not reacting quickly in that situation.“Monster Emperor, that bastard.” Xue Du was furious. Lin Wanyi was their greatest obstruction to entering the Rich Land. If they could clear him out, then everything would become simple.But the situation was not optimistic now.The Alliance had broken through Maigu City and had the advantage, but if they gathered many troops over here and Lin Wanyi attacked on his end and headed to the Alliance Headquarters, the damage caused wouldn’t be small.This was why the best plan for Alliance would be to take Lin Wanyi down.Suddenly, General Xue Du frowned, “Did Monster Emperor do it on purpose? He knew that he was Lin Wanyi’s son, so he took the risk to save him. Actually, Monster Emperor is allies with Lin Wanyi and is just acting like he is on our side?”Mao Shentai thought about it, “The possibility isn’t high. Don’t forget that his hands are covered with the blood of Maigu City. If he was on Lin Wanyi’s side, he definitely wouldn’t kill so many.”“No, I feel like it is really possible. No matter which side he is on, he is a lunatic. Other people can’t do it but he definitely can.” Xue Du started to suspect Monster Emperor.“Eh, that makes sense. We should be careful in the future and definitely can’t trust Monster Emperor. Let’s see what he wants.” Mao Shentai felt like Xue Du was right and he made a lot of sense.….You City Alliance Main Camp.Zhu Daoshen frowned. He heard the report from his men regarding the outcome of the situation from yesterday.“How many people want to leave?”That was right, Alliance members wanted to leave this place.“Many people. After last night, many of them collapsed psychologically like they had lost their souls and are not the same people anymore.” The reporting guy was really thin and wore spectacles. The eyes behind the glasses were really deep.“General, the cultivation of these technical people weren’t high and their wills were really weak. After the bug and sh*t rain, their wills have totally collapsed.”Zhu Daoshen kept silent for a long time, “I didn’t expect that we would have underestimated the natives to such a degree. Who knew that what so disgusting would actually produce such an outcome?”“Did anything else happen?”The male wearing glasses said, “Yes, many college students requested to go back. They said that something happened at home, so they shifted their holidays forwards and wanted to leave this place.”Pa!Zhu Daoshen smacked the table in rage and then clenched his fists, causing them to crack. He gritted his teeth, “Which colleges did they come from, they can’t take a bit of hardship? Did they forget their oath to fight for the Alliance when they entered the college?”The guy with glasses said, “General, this is only one of many. Who knew who it was who spread this on the Alliance platform? The Headquarters blocked it out, but it was too late; many people found out about it. When the Headquarters got the news, many people…”“Say it.” Zhu Daoshen’s expression was really ugly. Since such a thing happened, he would definitely be questioned.“They said that your ability was limited and wasn’t good enough to be here. They hoped that the Headquarters would move you back and send other generals over.” The guy lowered his head. He personally experienced the situation here and it was not a place that normal people could survive in.The scenes yesterday were too shocking.Even after a full day of cleaning, this place was still filled with a foul smell.As for the empty space outside, no one went there. Everyone hid in their rooms and didn’t come out. They were all afraid that something scary would drop from the sky.Zhu Daoshen took in a deep breath to relax. He couldn’t get angry because of this.That was true.You City had conned Alliance once and it was even by a kid who hadn’t even gone through puberty. That was such an insult to them.“General, actually we can return the favor to them. Since the natives did that, then why don’t we try?” The guy in glasses said.Zhu Daoshen stared at him such that he lowered his head and didn’t dare to look into his eyes.A long while later.Zhu Daoshen said coldly, “You want to start a sh*t war between us and the natives? After decades and centuries, what would be recorded in history is that our Alliance played sh*t with the natives?”“General, calm down. I didn’t consider this fully.” The guy lowered his head and admitted his mistake.That was indeed the case.The natives were shameless but they couldn’t be.Especially since he was the strongest general of Alliance.Zhu Daoshen waved his hand, “You can leave. Let whoever who wants to leave, leave. We will select people from the Headquarters. Also for those who want to leave, we will remove them from their original position, the college will expel them and the research members will be locked up. They will be treated like traitors.”“Yes, general.” The guy in glasses replied. He was going to send a message, for all members of the Alliance who went to attack the border know what the consequence of retreating was.But truthfully speaking, he felt like using the same method to deal with the enemy would be effective, but since the general didn’t agree, then there was nothing he could do.There was nothing he could do.….Liang Yichu’s mouth was agape. When he learned how Brother Lin used sh*t to teach Alliance a lesson, he was totally stunned.He could even think of such a way?There was also a general who was covered in shit who charged over in rage and then he was forced to flee in the end.What was going on?That was just too terrifying.He have never expected that such an unassuming thing would actually have such a strong killing ability.This was something that spread out around You City.Young Master Lin used the shit to cause Alliance to collapse. At the same time, he covered general Gang Xiong in shit.This caused a huge uproar.The people left in the city weren’t that strong and were basically below God Essence Realm.Of course, there were people from God Essence Realm, but there weren’t many. They stayed here to keep guard. If there were people sneaking over, they would inform them right away.“Oh my god, does this mean we have credit for that?”“Right, Young Master Lin said that we all played a part.”“Amazing, that is just so amazing.”“From the start, I thought that Liang Family was only cleaning that place to make the environment better. Who knew that there was such a use? Unfortunately, I wasn’t there and wasn’t able to witness those scenes.”The people in You City talked excitedly.They were all really interested in this matter.Someone led the way and actually started a sh*tting trend in order to contribute more in the battle against the alliance.Some drank cold water and then hot water to make their stomach hurt.Some were even worse.Some even ate beans.That day, You City started to spell, there were also rumbling noises. There were many long lines at the toilets.Some people queued many times a day, and many entered the toilets smiling, but left with ashen white faces.But they were proud and delighted.They were already satisfied to be able to play a part in fighting the Alliance.When Lin Fan found out about this, he was stunned.Oh my god.What were they thinking, they were just sick in the head.He was filled with disbelief. He even wanted to tell them to stop as that move wouldn’t work again.But he knew that he wouldn’t be able to stop it with his words.Since that was the case, then he could only allow them to continue.“Haiz, life is a little lonely sometimes.” Lin Fan laid on the chair and looked at the main camp. Even from so far away, it was like he could smell something. But he wouldn’t despise them all look at them in disdain, that was because they were still there and didn’t leave.Looking at the rage points, there weren’t many, only about 50,000.It was still very few, a pitiful amount.However, this wouldn’t be a problem; when the next battle started, it would be time for him to get more.A few days later.During these few days, battles broke out at other border cities.Fengyue City’s battle was the most intense.Qiu Dao was a terrifying expert, since he was there Fengyue City was really stable. But the Alliance was strong too; the generals they sent became stronger and stronger.Sword Head also met his mortal enemy. Actually, maybe it shouldn’t have been called that.To suppress him, the Alliance sent a sword user. His understanding of the sword was no less than the Sword Head.To the Alliance, the natives on the Rich Land were on an overall stronger level than them.But unfortunately, the natives in Rich Land weren’t united and fought among themselves.If they were united, then the Alliance really couldn’t do anything.At the start, Sword Head really didn’t pay much attention to the Alliance sword user, but as they fought more and more, he realized that his understanding was really in-depth and was no weaker than his.In the end, Sword Head released the Killing Sword Path.On that day, the Alliance and Fengyue City experts all sensed a terrifying killing intent spreading through the air.The Sword Head who had released himself was not the Sword Head from before. Although he was still really rational, he was now a killer; he was really vicious and each sword had to see blood.At that point, Qiu Dao’s face was ashen white and he was afraid that Sword Head would lose himself. If he entered a frenzied state, it would be catastrophic for Fengyue City.But luckily, he suppressed that rage and became rational.He managed to force the Alliance back and they were able to gain breathing space.King Wutong entered the three strongholds.Liu Xuan found out that King Wutong often woke up at night and found it hard to sleep. The maidservants all felt like there was something wrong with his body.But Liu Xuan just smiled like everything was within his control.Of course, he couldn’t manage to sleep.Entering the three strongholds was a joyous matter. If he was King Wutong, he wouldn’t be able to sleep too.What he didn’t know was that King Wutong didn’t find it hard to sleep because of that.It was because he didn’t know what to do next.His past experience couldn’t help him.Alliance’s cooperation totally brought him on a path of no return.“Brother, why don’t we just forget it. Let me return. I am under so much pressure.”King Wutong teared up secretly; he felt like this wasn’t a fake revolt but he was forced to revolt.What he was most afraid of was the Alliance envoy coming.That was because he didn’t know how to reply.Did he really have to fully immerse in the role, to let Alliance believe him? Did he have to step into the abyss, to make sacrifices for the big picture?He was not sure.He didn’t want to either.What was brother thinking?He couldn’t guess it and felt like something was wrong.What filled King Wutong with disbelief was that during this period of time, many sects joined their side. Although they were just some small and middle-tier sects, he noticed that Alliance’s strength here wasn’t weak.Maybe only the tip of the iceberg appeared. The truly terrifying things were still hidden in the darkness.….Lin Fan laid there and didn’t do anything. The Alliance not attacking made him quite lost in life.He wanted to find a different way to play with the Alliance.But what happened recently made them wary and it would be hard for him to succeed, which was why he had to wait.However, he thought of a way.Yin Demons were in the forest outside the city. They could help to fight against the Alliance.Although he couldn’t provoke them, if he disturbed them, maybe he would gain something.“Young Master…”A familiar voice spread into his ears.Lin Fan turned around and when he saw those two people, he got up and was a little stunned, “Why did you all come?”He didn’t expect Gou’zi and the nine-headed bug to come.Gou’zi walked over excitedly, “Young Master, after you left, I asked Feng Poliu where you went. He didn’t say anything, but then Nine Demon dragged me here.”Lin Fan thought Feng Poliu revealed it and wanted to say that he would teach him a lesson. What he didn’t expect was it was Nine Demon.He didn’t tell where he was going to Nine Demon.Moreover, Nine Demon didn’t know where You City was.Did it just rely on scent?“You bug..” Lin Fan pointed at it and wanted to say something, “Forget it, since you are back, then so be it. It feels a little weird without you serving by my side.”Gou’zi smiled happily, he had obtained Young master’s recognition.Nine Demon’s body was a little huge. Luckily, the city wall was wide and could fit it. Its nine heads shook about in delight.In the distance.“Brother Wu, that looks familiar.” Lin Wanyi asked.Old Wu said, “Old Master, this is Insect Valley’s Nine Demon. We saw the mother and was chased by it from south to north. If not for an expert saving us, we would have entered its stomach. ”Lin Wanyi frowned, “I feel like something is off. The kid is becoming really wild. If he attracts people from Insect Valley, it would be a huge headache.”“Old Master, what is wrong with this? I told you that Young Master going out was a good decision. He had so many fortuitous encounters, far more than we had.” Old Wu smiled.“Forget it, let him do what he wants. If Insect Valley really comes, we can make use of them, and maybe they can help us too. What is the situation with that bastard Su, did no top sects help?” Lin Wanyi asked.Old Wu shook his head, “No, we haven’t gotten a reply. Old Master, it seems like we can forget about it. Those who wanted to come have already come, those who didn’t were confirmed 20 years ago. They were all waiting for there to be a new reshuffling of powers.”“There is a chance that these sects signed an agreement with Alliance.”This conjecture was really possible.Lin Wanyi sighed and he felt tired. Why were there so many stupid people?If the Alliance really entered their land, the top sects might have good lives but those mid-tier sects might not.“Old Master, Zhao Family is great; they sent experts to defend other areas.” Old Wu said.Lin Wanyi said, “Can they not? They live beneath the foot of the Imperial Dynasty. If they try anything, they will be the first to get wiped out. Only they know where the dragon is and that is the promise that the Imperial Dynasty gave them. That is why they, more than anyone else, hope that the Alliance will never enter this place.”
