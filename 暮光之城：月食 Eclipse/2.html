　　Ridiculous.”

　　family — all committed to protecting human life — preferred the flavor of large predators for satisfying theirdietary needs. “Alaska, then, as planned. Only somewhere much more remote than Juneau — somewherewith grizzlies galore.”

　　 He kissed the top of my head and sighed.

　　“I’m not going along with that. I have to see Jacob.”

　　“Then I’ll have to stop you.”

　　He sounded utterly confident that this wouldn’t be a problem.
