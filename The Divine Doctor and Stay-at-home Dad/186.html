Chapter 186 Being in the Right and Self-confident Shamelessly

Nalan Wushuang said, “No problem. I’m sure I’ll offer you the lowest price.”Then they changed from good sisters to business partners. They negotiated cooperation matters one by one and finally signed the contract.Qin Haodong listened for a while and asked, “Is there anything about me? If it’s all right, I’m going to work.”“Isn’t the company yours? Why is it none of your business?” Nalan Wushuang cast a stern glance at him and said, “Why you don’t want to be a boss but a worker?”Qin Haodong said, “I’m a little doctor. It’s best for you to take charge of the company’s affairs.”Ouyang Shanshan said, “Doctor Qin, I want to invite you and your daughter to join my concert tomorrow. Would you?”“That’s good. Of course we are willing to come to your concert.”Qin Haodong did not hesitate to accept the invitation. First, he was willing to let his child participate in more activities. Second, Ouyang Shanshan was the idol of the little fellow. It was absolutely the most delightful thing for his daughter to attend the concert of Ouyang Shanshan.“But the concert is tomorrow night. Is it too late now?”“That’s all right. Tang Tang’s singing talent is excellent. You will send her to my company tomorrow morning. We will rehearse for one day and then we can play in the evening.”“Well, it’s a deal,” Ouyang Shanshan said and took out two VIP tickets and sent them to Qin Haodong. “Here are the tickets for the concert. Come and see us with Miss Lin.”“OK, we are sure to go!” Qin Haodong said that and he reached out to get the tickets, but Nalan Wushuang took the tickets first.”“Just give Miss Lin a ticket. You don’t need it,” Nalan Wushuang said. Then she took back one of the tickets and gave the other to Qin Haodong.“Qin Haodong said in surprise, “Why don’t I need a ticket? Do I look like a child? Can I get a free ticket?”Nalan Wushuang looked at him with an eerie smile and said, “Because you are the security guard of our company, you just follow the guard to maintain order. There is no time for you to enjoy the show.”“Well… you said you will let me be a standing guard?” Qin Haodong asked with a bitter expression.Nalan Wushuang laughed and said, “Of course. We are short of manpower in such a large concert. Don’t forget that you are also a member of the company. You said that you will obey my instructions.”“Well… All right then.”Qin Haodong could only accept. At this time, he regretted very much. He would let Ma Wenzhuo’s men leave two days later if he had known this.After discussing the concert, Nalan Wushuang and Ouyang Shanshan continued to talk about fashion together.Qin Haodong drove to work in Lin’s Group. Near the end of work, he received a phone call from Wang Rubing.After the call was put through, Wang Rubing cried, “You brat. Why don’t you call me for so many days? Did you forget your sister since you have a girlfriend?”“Well… Sister, I’ve been too busy lately…”Qin Haodong was really embarrassed. He wanted to take his sister to his home, but these days there were so many things that he forgot Wang Rubing when he was busy.“Then tell me what the hell are you about?”Qin Haodong said, “Actually, they are not big things. There are too many mosquitoes in Jiangnan City recently. I’m responsible for expelling them. Besides, the whole world is warming. I’m insulating Antarctica. Otherwise, the icebergs are really going to melt. We’ll sell houses and buy boats at that time…”Zhang Zhijie just took a sip of tea. Hearing his serious nonsense, Zhang Zhijie sprayed it out with amusement.“Okay, I have no time to listen to your nonsense!” Wang Rubing interrupted Qin Haodong directly and said, “No matter what great thing you have, you will go to dinner with me tonight.”“Dinner? Where are you going to have dinner?” Qin Haodong asked.“I’ve already registered at the Sanitary Bureau. The section chief invited me to dinner today. You’ll come with me.”“Sister, your colleague invites you to dinner. Is it not appropriate for me to go with you?” Qin Haodong said.“What a colleague. I don’t think he has good intentions. He doesn’t look at me in the right way. I’m afraid he has a bad plan if I go alone.”Hearing this, Qin Haodong immediately became interested. “How old are your section chief?” he asked.“Well, I don’t know. It looks like he is less than 30.”“Is he handsome or not?”“It’s OK. He’s not ugly.” Wang Rubing suddenly understood and cried, “Why are you asking this?”Qin Haodong said, “Isn’t that just right?” “You know, my aunt is urging you to find a boyfriend. Although he is a little older, it is barely suitable for you. You can think about it.”“Think about what? He’s been married for years and his son can run.”“Oh!” Hearing that man was married and still pestered Wang Rubing, Qin Haodong’s face suddenly turned cold. “Sister, where are you now? I’ll pick you up.”“Come on, I’ll wait for you at the door of the Sanitary Bureau.”Then Wang Rubing hung up the phone.Qin Haodong called Lin Momo and asked her to pick up the little fellow, and then drove directly to the Sanitary Bureau. When he arrived at the door of the Sanitary Bureau, Wang Rubing, dressed in a white dress, was waiting there.After seeing Lamborghini Centenario, Wang Rubing said in surprise, “Dong, you really have a rich girlfriend. This is the car she bought for you?”After Wang Rubing was asked to get on the bus, Qin Haodong said, “What did you say? Although I can earn money by my face, I have always relied on my ability. I bought this car by my own without others’ help.”“What a beautiful car!” Wang Rubing stroked Lamborghini’s luxurious interior trim and said, “Dong, you really are capable. Your sister is not good like you. I can’t afford a sports car with my salary even without eating or drinking for a lifetime.”“That’s easy. I give you one tomorrow.”There was a Porsche 911 in Qin Haodong’s house. He decided to give it to Wang Rubing, although he said seriously, Wang Rubing took it as a joke and did not really take it seriously.Starting the car, he asked, “Where shall we go?”Wang Rubing said, “The section chief said the hotel was named Dreamy Jiangnan. He has booked a room there.”“Oh, I see.”Qin Haodong had been to Dreamy Jiangnan Hotel twice in recent days. Naturally, he was very familiar with it and drove directly there.After arriving at the hotel, they were led by the waiter to an ordinary private room. After all, the lowest consumption of VIP rooms with balconies which Qin Haodong had come before was at least more than 100 thousand. It was not affordable for ordinary people.The waiter politely opened the door and Wang Rubing and Qin Haodong went in together.“Rubing, you’re here!”In the private room sat a young man, wearing a pair of golden glasses and a suitable suit. He looked very gentle and handsome indeed, but his eyes looked very obscene. He was the section chief named Niu Fei, as Wang Rubing said.When he saw Wang Rubing, he was excited at first. But then he saw Qin Haodong following her, and his face suddenly turned cold.“Rubing, I mean I want to have dinner with you. Who is this man?”Wang Rubing said, “Chief Niu, this is my cousin Qin Haodong. He just had no place to eat at night, so I let him come with me.”She had been mentally prepared to introduce Qin Haodong to Niu Fei.Hearing that it was Wang Rubing’s cousin, Niu Fei’s face suddenly relaxed a lot and he said, “Oh, that’s it. Have a sit quickly.”Qin Haodong did not say much. He sat down next to Wang Rubing.“Little Qin, where do you work?” Niu Fei asked.“I’m a senior student in Jiangnan Medical College, and I haven’t graduated yet.”Qin Haodong had come here twice in a row to the VIP room, but he had not been to this kind of ordinary room, so he looked around as he talked. Compared with the VIP room in the Dreamy Jiangnan Hotel, this kind of ordinary room’s decoration was really at a lower level. It was still very luxurious compared with the ordinary hotel.However, in Niu Fei’s eyes, Qin Haodong was a bumpkin who had never seen such a luxurious decoration. His eyes showed a trace of disdain and he proudly said, “Little Qin, as you haven’t graduated yet, no wonder you haven’t been to such an upscale place. Let me tell you, this Dreamy Jiangnan Hotel is absolutely the top-grade place in Jiangnan City. Anyway, we will spend thousands of yuan for a meal here. Ordinary people can’t afford it at all.Qin Haodong just smiled and did not speak. He was accompanying Wang Rubing this time. As long as this guy did not have bad intentions to Wang Rubing, he was too lazy to pay attention to him.Soon, the waiter brought the drinks and dishes to the room. They began to have dinner.Niu Fei opened a bottle of red wine and frequently advised Wang Rubing to drink. Although Wang Rubing was somehow good at drinking, she did not drink with anyone. No matter how Niu Fei advised her, she just tasted it lightly. But Qin Haodong drank very happily. Soon, most of the bottle of red wine entered his stomach.Niu Fei stared at Qin Haodong discontentedly and said, “Little Qin, I think you have eaten enough food. Can you go out for a walk? I have a few words to talk to your cousin alone.”In his opinion, as long as Qin Haodong was sensible, he would leave the private room. While Qin Haodong just bowed his head and ate. He said vaguely, “Chief Niu, you can just say what you want. I just eat dinner here. You can just take I am not here.”“You…”Niu Fei stared at Qin Haodong fiercely with a disgust expression.Wang Rubing said, “Chief Niu, my cousin grew up with me when he was young. Our relationship is the best. You can tell me what you want.”Niu Fei thought about it, and no longer hid his intention. He said directly, “Rubing, to tell you the truth, I fell in love with you since the day you came in and I wanted you to be my lover.”Qin Haodong still bowed his head and ate, but his eyes showed a cold light. This guy really had bad intentions to his cousin.Wang Rubing did not expect Niu Fei to make such a request directly. She said with a cold expression, “Chief Niu, don’t you already have a wife and a son?”“For men, marriage is marriage, family is family, love is love,” Niu Fei eloquently said, “What I value is you. Although I can’t marry you, you can believe that my feelings for you are absolutely true!”Qin Haodong was completely shocked. He had never seen a man could speak so shamelessly and rightly of a shame request before. How barefaced this man was!Niu Fei continued and said, “You won’t lose if you are willing to be my lover. You are still on probation. I can assure you that you will become a full member as soon as possible and that you will have a good development in the future. At the same time, after your brother graduated, I also will arrange a job for him. All hospitals in Jiangnan City are free to choose, as well as government departments. He can go wherever he wants.”Wang Rubing said, “Chief Niu, these are not what I want, and I can’t answer your request.”Seeing that he was rejected for the second time, Niu Fei changed his expression and said, “Wang Rubing, don’t forget that you are only a poor student from a small county town. You will have no future in Jiangnan City without background, and money.”“Be my lover. I promise you that you will have a good life, a smooth sailing, and a good job for your brother. If you refuse a toast only to drink a forfeit, not only will you not be able to become a full member, but your brother will not be able to graduate from Jiangnan Medical College.”
